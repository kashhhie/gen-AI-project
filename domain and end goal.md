# Domain and End Goal

## Domain of the Project
The domain of this project is **Operations Management and Industrial Engineering**, with a strong emphasis on **Process Improvement and Lean Management**. The project is applicable across both **manufacturing and service industries**, including production systems, supply chain operations, administrative workflows, and service delivery processes.

Key domain areas include:
- Operations Management  
- Lean Manufacturing  
- Process Optimization  
- Quality Management  
- Productivity Improvement  
- Business Process Reengineering  

This project integrates analytical tools and continuous improvement methodologies to enhance operational efficiency and reduce waste.

## End Goal of the Project
The primary end goal of this project is to **reduce the overall process cycle time** by identifying inefficiencies, eliminating non-value-added activities, and optimizing workflow performance.

Specific end goals include:
- Achieving faster process completion without compromising quality  
- Improving throughput and operational efficiency  
- Minimizing delays, waiting time, and bottlenecks  
- Enhancing resource utilization and cost efficiency  
- Increasing customer satisfaction through timely delivery  
- Establishing a standardized and sustainable process improvement framework  

The project aims to deliver measurable improvements that can be replicated and scaled across similar processes within the organization.
